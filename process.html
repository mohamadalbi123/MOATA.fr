Process

From business details to a live assistant.

A simple setup flow for creating, testing, and adding the AI diagnostic assistant to your website.

01

Business setup

Add your business information.

Start with your business name, industry, website, location, booking link, and contact details.

02

Assistant style

Choose how the assistant appears.

Select the assistant style, name, brand colors, and logo so it feels connected to your business.

03

Services and questions

Add what you offer and what you usually ask.

Enter your services, prices, duration, and recommendation notes. Then choose suggested question cards or add your own questions.

04

Preview

Test the assistant before launch.

Complete the diagnostic like a customer. If the recommendation is not right, edit the services, questions, or rules and test again.

05

Launch

Add it to your website.

Use one universal embed code, a popup, an inline widget, an iframe, or a standalone hosted page.

Ready to start?

Build your assistant.

Start with your business details, services, normal customer questions, and the rules the assistant should follow.
