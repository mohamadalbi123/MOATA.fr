Platform overview

Start building your personalized AI diagnostic assistant.

MOATA helps your business ask customers the right questions, understand what they need, and recommend the services you offer. Your team saves time, customers choose with confidence, and the appointment starts better prepared.

Personalize the assistant

Choose the industry, avatar, launch style, and customer experience that fits your business.

  • Industry-specific setup
  • Male or female assistant avatar
  • Professional or casual appearance
  • Photo upload when needed
  • Standalone link or website widget

Teach it your services

Add the services or products you offer, then define when each one should be recommended.

  • Service names and descriptions
  • Prices, duration, and booking links
  • Rules for each recommendation
  • Things the assistant must avoid
  • No invented services

Guide customers before they contact you

Customers answer, upload if needed, receive a recommendation, and move toward the correct booking step.

  • Better prepared appointments
  • Fewer repeated calls and chats
  • More confident customers
  • Cleaner booking decisions
  • Customer answers ready for your team
Build My Assistant

Current version

The builder collects the setup. MOATA prepares the assistant.

The current build page lets a business choose its industry, avatar, questions, photo settings, launch style, services, and rules.

Preview, checkout, automatic AI generation, and the final embed code are the next SaaS step. For now, MOATA uses the submitted setup to prepare the first assistant manually.

Core rule

Preparation is key before the appointment.

MOATA gives customers a simple way to explain their need before they call or book. Your team receives clearer context, and the customer is guided toward the exact service they should consider.

The assistant uses your business information as the source of truth. It should only recommend services you actually offer, and if it is unsure, it sends the customer to contact you.
