Use case

How an AI diagnostic assistant helps a beauty salon guide clients.

Lazoya is a beauty salon in France with many services, including advanced treatments that clients do not always know how to choose. The assistant gives clients a guided way to explain their needs, answer questions, upload a photo when useful, and reach the right service before booking.

01

Client need

Clients wanted help choosing the right treatment.

Some services were new or advanced, so clients needed guidance before making a confident booking decision.

02

Questions

The assistant asks what the team normally asks.

Clients answer guided questions about their concern, goals, history, sensitivity, and other details needed before recommending.

03

Photo

Photos can give useful visual context.

When useful, the client can upload a photo so the assistant has more context before suggesting a service.

04

Recommendation

The assistant recommends from the real offer only.

The recommendation is based on Lazoya's actual services, with a simple explanation of why that service fits.

05

Booking

The client reaches the right booking step.

After the recommendation, the assistant sends the client toward the correct booking path instead of leaving them unsure.

Product value

Less repeated explaining. More confident clients.

80% less manual time spent understanding repeated client needs by phone or chat.
50% increase in bookings after clients had a clearer way to choose and move forward.
