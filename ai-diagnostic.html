AI diagnostic assistant

Understand customer needs before the appointment.

Customers answer questions, upload photos when needed, and receive a recommended service or product from your real offer.

What it solves

Less confusion, fewer wrong bookings, better prepared appointments.

Many customers hesitate because they do not know which service, product, treatment, or appointment type is right for them. The assistant asks the questions your team normally asks before recommending.

It helps the customer explain the situation before booking, then guides them to the right next step without replacing the professional.

Launch as a popup, inline widget, floating button, iframe, or standalone hosted page.

Safety rules

The assistant guides. It does not replace the professional.

For sensitive industries, MOATA works as a pre-consultation or intake assistant. It should never diagnose disease, promise results, or recommend anything outside your real business offer.
