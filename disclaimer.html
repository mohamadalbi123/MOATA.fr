AI Disclaimer

Last updated: July 22, 2026

AI Is Assistive

MOATA assistants are designed to help customers explain their needs and guide them toward a relevant service or product offered by a business. AI output is informational and assistive. It may be wrong, incomplete, or not suitable for a specific situation.

Not Professional Advice

MOATA does not provide medical, dental, veterinary, legal, financial, tax, engineering, construction, electrical, plumbing, emergency, or safety-critical advice. For those industries, the assistant should only recommend the appropriate appointment or service category and encourage customer review by a qualified professional.

No Emergencies

MOATA assistants are not emergency services. If a customer has severe symptoms, immediate danger, unsafe property conditions, fire risk, flooding, electrical hazards, legal deadlines, or any urgent situation, they should contact the business directly, emergency services, or a qualified professional immediately.

Business Review Required

Businesses are responsible for reviewing their assistant setup, service list, questions, restrictions, language, and public recommendations before sharing the assistant with customers. Businesses should update the assistant whenever services, prices, policies, or booking rules change.

Customer Responsibility

Customers should treat AI recommendations as a helpful starting point only. Final service suitability, appointment type, safety checks, pricing, and professional decisions must be confirmed by the business or qualified professional.
