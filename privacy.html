Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Information We Collect

We may collect account details, name, email, business name, phone number, WhatsApp number, website, city, location, booking URL, selected industry, assistant setup choices, service lists, business rules, brand color, language, and messages you send to us.

When customers use a diagnostic assistant, the assistant may collect answers, preferences, contact details, photos, uploaded files, notes, and generated recommendations depending on how the business configured it.

How We Use Information

We use information to create and manage accounts, build assistants, generate recommendations, display hosted diagnostic pages, provide embed code, send notifications, process payments, provide support, improve the platform, and protect the service from misuse.

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe or another payment provider. MOATA does not store full payment card numbers. Payment providers may collect billing details and payment information under their own privacy terms.

AI Processing

Customer answers and business configuration may be sent to AI service providers to generate recommendations. We aim to send only the information needed for the assistant to work.

Service Providers

We may use providers for hosting, authentication, database storage, file storage, payments, email delivery, AI processing, analytics, scheduling, and security. These providers process information only as needed to support MOATA.

Business Responsibility

Businesses using MOATA are responsible for telling their own customers how customer data is collected and used, especially when collecting health, legal, financial, location, image, or other sensitive information.

Data Retention

We keep information as long as needed to provide the platform, maintain records, comply with obligations, resolve disputes, and improve the service. You may request deletion where legally available.

Your Choices

You can contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or export of information where applicable. Some information may need to be kept for legal, security, billing, or fraud-prevention reasons.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No internet service is completely secure, so businesses should avoid collecting unnecessary sensitive information.

Children

MOATA is designed for business use and is not intended for children. Businesses should not configure assistants to collect information from children unless they have the legal right to do so.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact page.
